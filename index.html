About Water Street Vintage

Water Street Vintage & Bohemian is a local vintage shop in Harrisonburg, VA. It features clothing and accessories from the 60's and 70's. The items are found through vintage clothing warehouses and local or online auctions picked by the owner.

Water Street Vintage Story

Water Street Vintage and Bohemian was founded by Mike Harlow who first thought about opening up a vintage store after he was inspired to start wearing 60's and 70's style again. When he started getting some attention for the way he dressed and people wanted to know where they could get clothes like that. The store is filled with items that Harlow hand picks that he loves and thinks others will too!
